Privacy & Security Policy

How we protect your data

AWS Credentials Handling

  • Session-Only Storage: Your AWS credentials are stored ONLY in encrypted session memory
  • Never Logged: Credentials are NEVER written to logs, databases, or files
  • Never Transmitted: Credentials are NEVER sent to third parties
  • Auto-Clear: Credentials are automatically cleared after 30 minutes of inactivity
  • Complete Logout: All credential data is completely destroyed on logout

Data Privacy

  • User Isolation: You can ONLY view results from your own uploads
  • No Data Sharing: Your log data is never shared with other users
  • Your AWS Resources: Files are uploaded to YOUR AWS S3 bucket using YOUR credentials
  • No Third Parties: This application uses NO external services, CDNs, or analytics

Security Measures

  • CSRF Protection: All forms are protected against cross-site request forgery
  • Rate Limiting: Protection against brute-force attacks
  • Session Fingerprinting: Detection of session hijacking attempts
  • Input Sanitization: All inputs are validated and sanitized
  • Security Headers: Comprehensive HTTP security headers on all responses
  • No External Resources: All assets are served locally - no CDNs or external fonts

Your Responsibilities

  • Keep your AWS credentials secure and private
  • Use credentials with minimal required permissions
  • Only upload log files you are authorized to analyze
  • Log out when finished to clear your credentials
  • Do not share your session with others

Contact

For security concerns, please contact your system administrator.
